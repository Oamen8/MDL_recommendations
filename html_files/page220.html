Well-meaning but socially awkward, Kuronuma Sawako is nicknamed "Sadako" by her high school classmates because of her similar hair style with the main character from the horror movie "Ring." She doesn't have friends and goes about her day in quiet. She admires a fellow classmate, Kazehaya Shota, because of his easy-going and friendly nature, but unknown to her, Kazehaya has also been paying attention to her. (Source: MyDramaList) ~~ Adapted from the manga "Kimi ni Todoke: From Me to You" (君に届け) by Shiina Karuho (椎名軽穂). Edit Translation

While watching the movie I felt as if I turned to my old self and it felt amazing and sad at the same time even if it was only for a short period of time. The actors were very good and they portrayed the characters very well. I strongly recommend this movie when you have lost any hope of finding your "price charming". It will make you dream! :)
