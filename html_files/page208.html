Zuo Zai Jun and Zheng Lei were university classmates and best friends, who have an 8-year-old step-daughter called Miao Miao. Miao Miao’s mother, Ning Xiao Meng, was Zai Jun and Zheng Lei’s dream girl during their university days. 8 years ago, she left to study in America and was never heard from again. 8 years later, Xiao Meng returns to Shanghai. Zai Jun cannot accept her so she attempts to mend their relationship. When she succeeds, Xiao Meng becomes very close to Zheng Lei, ruining the friendship between the men as they become love rivals creating comedic situations. Little by little, Xiao Meng’s secret about Miao Miao’s birth and her disappearance is unraveled. Edit Translation

Once in a while you accidentally come across some hidden treasures of narration with some beautiful, heartrenching music and cozy,warm cinematography.The bromance between the two male leads (mind you,2 male leads indeed as you never feel any injustice of character- development has been done to any of the 2 male characters) takes precedence over the underlying theme of romance.The story proves that love between 2 humans can be shared between 3 and that is enough to last for an eternity despite time-tested changes in the isosceles triangle.40 episodes may appear like a drag to some but I never felt betrayed by the story till the end.However I would have preferred if the underhanded tactics of the other female character could have been omitted from the story-line altogether.First love's beauty with dignity and Grace did not really call for those.

If you like family drama, you might enjoy this drama. As for me, I have mixed feelings on this drama. I like the story and the two male leads and the little girl (She's the same little girl from Two Fathers, a Taiwanese drama that I've watched), but I really do not like the main female lead. Her acting is bad for portraying someone who's diagnosed with a life threatening disease. It's like she doesn't show any emotions, her emotions when the disease part is not revealed until we know she has that disease is the same. This drama is actually enjoyable, I like the family and friendship value, but for me (other people may feel different - this is just me) the main female lead ruined the show for me.
