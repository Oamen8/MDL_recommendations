At night in Tokyo, Midnight, an outcast taxi driver, meets Kaede, a girl who was driving a dekotora. When Midnight learns that Kaede's life is being targeted by a rival company for some reason, he decides to help Kaede escape. He races through neon streets and engages in car chases in a modified taxi with a "fifth wheel" that allows him to drive on any road. (Source: natalie.mu) ~~ Adapted from the manga series "Midnight' (ミッドナイト) by Osamu Tezuka (手塚 治虫) Edit Translation
