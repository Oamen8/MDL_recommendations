Tang Nannan, an aspiring writer and web blogger with an imaginative mind, accidentally ruins Zhu Huo's proposal to his ideal woman, making him her enemy. Despite their differences, they end up cohabiting. Meanwhile, Zhu Huo’s charming friend, Xiao Jian Jun, falls for Nannan and starts pursuing her. As tensions rise between the three, Zhu Huo's jealousy stirs conflict, setting the stage for a complicated romance full of misunderstandings and unexpected connections. A nice romantic comedy that has the feeling of a webtoon. The story is
okay, kind of cliché, but entertaining nevertheless. The comedy factor
was overstretched, as the events were kind of ridiculous, though they
produced laughter, so the film makers succeeded (this could have been a
lot worse if they hadn't handled it well). In addition, the creepy,
unrealistic body part changes reminded me of those manga movies that
try to follow the manga on which they are based on to the point of
being idiotic. That said, the plot had a strong feeling of being a
manga-webtoon as well, as one event finished and right away a new
character popped up out of nowhere and kind of expected the viewer to
know him. Finally, the performances were good and the chemistry between
the two actors strong enough to produce a good romance. So, 4 out of
10.
