Soo Jung has been with her boyfriend for 10 years, and it’s clear to everyone but her that their romance is going nowhere. On a business trip to Busan by train, she sits next to Jae Hyun whose flirting leaves her indignant and increasingly flustered. She tries to push him away but ends up sharing a car ride to Busan with him when the train breaks down. During their impromptu road trip, she discovers that there might be more to Jae Hyun than first appeared. Edit Translation

Such a great feel good movie. Really light fun story, with a great cast, and set in the beautiful city of Busan. It has just enough drama to make your heart squeeze, and the characters and the relationships are developed at a great pace. The one night stand is a common rom-com theme, but I think what sets these movies apart from one another is the way they make that brief encounter meaningful. I think the movie was successful in adding depth in a way that makes the development in the story, particularly the characters' relationship, believable.

Definitely worth your time!

It was funny, cute and lovely to see both Moon Chae Won and Yoo Yeon Suk acting together.. I'm big fan of both actors and their work. And I think that their pairing was a pure gold.
The impression that this movie left on me was good.
But as for the story, it's already seen. Also it's really risky to do a rom/com movie based on a topic of a one night stand, because in a real life one night stands are strongly disliked and don't have any relation with romance.
But on the other side I think that they made a serious topic, that is like a big thing nowadays, interesting and that they pointed out the problem that is very common and normal today in our society.
As for the acting... Well, there's nothing to say about it. I mean, Moon Chae Won and Yoo Yeon Suk are both great actors and I can only say "Bravo!!!!". Hahahaha. XD
The chemistry between the leads was amazing. And I would like to see another project with this two actors.
So here, in this movie, the writer is giving us the opportunity to see a good and a bad side of people. Of course, one is a bad side or should I say a cold side which people use as a wall to protect themselves from being hurt and the other is good, soft and warm side which a person rarely shows to the other people.
As the movie goes on, they slowly begin to develop a feeling towards each other, as they get the opportunity to see good qualities of that other person.
OST.. Well, I liked it. But also I didn't pay that much attention on the OST, cause I was more focused on the theme of a movie and chemistry between the leads.
Overall, it was refreshing to watch a movie like this, with this kind of a cast.
And I recommend watching it, cause in the end you will end up liking it.
